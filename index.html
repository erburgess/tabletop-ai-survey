Community Survey · 2025

AI & Creativity in
Tabletop Game Design

We're gathering honest perspectives from across the tabletop gaming community on AI, creativity, and the future of game design. Your voice matters — this survey takes about five minutes.

A note on terminology: This survey focuses on generative AI — tools that produce original text, images, audio, or other creative content from prompts (e.g. ChatGPT, Midjourney, Claude, Stable Diffusion). We are not asking about corrective AI (grammar tools), machine learning used in analytics, or algorithmic systems that don't directly replace or replicate human creative output.
